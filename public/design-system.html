Usage Guidelines

Brand Voice

  • DO Friendly, warm, encouraging
  • DO Simple language (many users are non-native English)
  • DO Use emojis sparingly for warmth
  • DON'T Corporate or cold tone
  • DON'T Technical jargon
  • DON'T Assume English fluency

Accessibility

  • Minimum touch target: 44x44px (Apple standard)
  • Text contrast: 4.5:1 minimum (WCAG AA)
  • Focus states visible on all interactive elements
  • All images have alt text
  • Support right-to-left for Arabic
